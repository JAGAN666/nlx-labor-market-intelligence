Skill Ecosystems

A structured, topographic map of the Colorado labor market. This visualization reveals how isolated skill domains connect across industries.

How to Read This Map

  1. The Ring: The top 120 most important skills in the state are arranged in a massive circle.
  2. Colored Domains: Skills are grouped into contiguous colored block "Ecosystems" based on how exclusively they correlate in job postings.
  3. The Web: The lines crossing the center show cross-industry requirements. Thicker lines equal tighter correlations.
  4. Interaction: Hover your mouse over any skill text to instantly highlight its specific network and fade the noise.
